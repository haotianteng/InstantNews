## Codebase Patterns

- **mypy config**: `pyproject.toml` has `[tool.mypy] ignore_missing_imports = true` — needed because many deps lack stubs (boto3, firebase_admin, etc.)
- **database.py kwargs**: Use `dict[str, Any]` for engine kwargs to avoid mypy narrowing errors when mixing bool/int/type values
- **Script conventions**: Scripts in `scripts/` use `sys.path.insert(0, ...)` + `dotenv` load + `init_db(Config.DATABASE_URL)` + `get_session()` pattern
- **News.link is unique**: The `link` column is the unique identifier for articles across local and production DBs
